About Red Line

Red Line Inspections provides comprehensive inspection services including Project Management, QA/QC, vendor inspections and third party inspection services to the structural, piping and civil construction industries. Our certified inspectors bring unparalleled expertise and experience to ensure vendor supplies meet project quality requirements, proper documentation is provided and technical support is delivered as needed.

Our founder, John Edward Young brings over 15 years of experience as a professional welder and inspector in both facility and shop work. Guided by his expertise, all inspection services completed by Redline Inspections exceed customer expectations and deliver fast, competent and reliable service. We pride ourselves on our professionalism, tried and true know-how and our prairie roots commitment to hard work and dedication.
